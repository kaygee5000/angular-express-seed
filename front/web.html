Ride with Us : "Make we go"!

Mo'Go is a mobile platform that reduces transportation cost throughout the sharing economy. Tell us where you want to go, and we will match you with a community driver. All over the world, the sharing economy is changing transportation in cities making it cheaper and more efficient for citizens. Mo'Go brings the sharing economy to Accra so you can also cut your cost and save more money.
